STUDY

Basic Quantum Information Theory


The study covers the basic contents of quantum information science, in line with the purpose of the quantum information science academic club.
We start with what Qubit is, and approach it from a mathematical/physical point of view how we treat it theoretically.
This study utilizes lecture materials made by the club members.
There are a total of seven lectures, and after taking the lecture, you will solve the quiz.

1st: Linear Algebra
2nd: The principles of quantum mechanics
3rd: Digital systems & Time complexity
4th: Qubit, Bloch Sphere, Tensor product
5th: Quantum Algorithm
6th: Quantum Key Distribution
7th: Density Matrix
